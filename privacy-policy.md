
# Privacy Policy for Uwingo Academy - Car Tracking System

**Last Updated:** 26.03.2026

At **Uwingo Academy**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Car Tracking System application handles information.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
Our application is designed to track vehicle locations. 
* **Location Data:** The app collects location data to provide tracking features. This data is used solely for the functionality of the app.
* **Personal Information:** We do not require you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the basic functions of the app.

### 2. Third-Party Access
**We do not share, sell, or trade your data with third parties.** * There are no third-party advertisements.
* There are no third-party analytics tools.
* Your data stays within the application environment.

### 3. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us your location information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
This application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
